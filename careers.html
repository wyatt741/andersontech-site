Commission the facilities the world runs on.

Anderson Technologies is building its commissioning team across Arizona and California. We are looking for engineers and technicians who take ownership of the field and the documentation on data center and mission critical construction projects.

Where you fit

We hire across the commissioning lifecycle, from field technicians to lead engineers. Every role owns both the testing and the record.

Commissioning Engineer

Lead functional and integrated systems testing for electrical and mechanical systems. Develop and execute test scripts, resolve field issues, and own the commissioning record through turnover.

Apply

Commissioning Technician

Support pre-functional checks, startup, and functional testing in the field. Record results, operate equipment during testing, and coordinate with trades and vendors.

Apply

Field Commissioning Agent

Represent the owner on site. Verify installation against design, witness startup and functional testing, and drive systems to energization and turnover.
