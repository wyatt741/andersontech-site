About

Independent commissioning, built on field discipline.

Anderson Technologies LLC commissions the electrical and mechanical systems behind data centers and other mission critical facilities. We are vendor independent, which means our only job is to prove your facility works, not to defend an install.

Our approach

Verify what was designed. Prove it survives failure.

Every project comes down to two questions. Is what got built the same as what was designed, and does it perform when something goes wrong. We answer both with a documented trail, from submittal review to integrated systems testing.

We treat your commissioning record like it will be read years from now, because it will be, by the operations team keeping the site alive.
