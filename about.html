About

Independent commissioning for mission critical construction.

Anderson Technologies LLC commissions the electrical and mechanical systems installed during data center and mission critical construction. As an independent, vendor neutral agent, our role is to verify performance on behalf of the owner.

Our approach

Verify the installation. Prove the performance.

Every project reduces to two questions. Does the installed work match the design, and does each system perform to its sequence of operations under load. We answer both with a documented trail from submittal review through integrated systems testing.

The commissioning record we deliver is built to support audit, warranty, and long term operations.
