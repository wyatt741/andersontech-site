Services

Commissioning across the construction lifecycle

Independent, script based commissioning for every system a data center depends on, from submittal review through integrated systems testing.

Electrical

Medium voltage switchgear, UPS, generators, ATS and STS, paralleling, load bank and functional testing, power distribution and busway.

Mechanical

Chillers, CRAC and CRAH units, cooling distribution units, pumps, airflow and thermal verification, and sequence of operations validation.

Integrated Testing

Integrated systems testing, test script development and review, specification and submittal conflict resolution, and turnover documentation.
