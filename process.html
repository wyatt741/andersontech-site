Process

Commissioning Levels 1 through 5.

Commissioning is not a punch list. It is a staged verification program that follows every critical system from the factory floor to full load failure testing. Level 0 design review comes first when we are engaged early; these five levels carry the build from there.

1
Level 1

Factory Witness Testing

Critical equipment is tested at the factory before it ships. We witness, verify against specification, and clear it for the site.

What we do

  • Factory acceptance tests witnessed against spec
  • Deviations documented and driven to resolution before shipment
  • Test records captured into the commissioning tracker
2
Level 2

Installation Verification

Equipment arrives and gets set. We verify the installation matches the design before anything is energized.

What we do

  • Receipt and condition inspection
  • Installation checked against drawings and manufacturer requirements
  • Pre energization inspections and clearance
3
Level 3

Pre Functional and Startup

Systems come to life for the first time, under supervision and against a checklist.

What we do

  • Pre functional checklists executed per asset
  • Vendor startup witnessed and verified
  • Point to point controls verification
4
Level 4

Functional Performance Testing

Each system is proven against its sequence of operations, individually, under load.

What we do

  • Script based functional testing per system
  • Load bank testing for the power path
  • Thermal performance for the cooling path
  • Failure and alarm response per the SOO
5
Level 5

Integrated Systems Testing

The whole facility runs together and gets pushed until it proves itself. Utility loss, failover, recovery.

What we do

  • Facility wide IST under design load
  • Utility failure and generator failover scenarios
  • Ride through and recovery documented
  • The final proof before live load
