Services

Full discipline commissioning, verified in the field.

Independent, script based commissioning across every system that keeps a mission critical facility online. We work the whole lifecycle, from submittal review to integrated systems testing, and we document every step for turnover.

The power path, verified from the utility to the rack.

  • Controls and BMS point to point checks

How we work

The commissioning lifecycle

Commissioning is not a single test at the end. It is a chain of checks that starts before anything is installed and ends when the facility is proven and documented.

01

Submittal Review

Mine the specs and cross check every submittal before procurement.

02

Factory Witness

Verify equipment against spec at the factory, before it ships.

03

Construction Verification

Confirm installation is correct, complete, and ready to energize.

04

Pre-Energization

Inspect and clear each asset before it is brought to life.

05

Functional Testing

Prove each system performs to its sequence of operations.

06

Integrated Testing

Run the whole facility through failure modes under load.
