Services

Commissioning services for data center construction.

Independent, script based commissioning for every system a mission critical facility depends on. We work the full construction lifecycle, from submittal review through integrated systems testing, and document each phase for turnover.

Electrical Commissioning

Power distribution from the utility service through the rack.

  • Medium voltage switchgear and transformers
  • UPS systems, batteries, and static transfer switches
  • Generators, paralleling gear, and load bank testing
  • Automatic transfer switches and distribution
  • Busway, panelboards, and branch circuits
  • Construction verification through functional testing

Mechanical and Cooling

Cooling systems verified under design load conditions.

  • Chillers, condenser water, and heat rejection
  • CRAC and CRAH units and cooling distribution units
  • Pumps, valves, and hydronic systems
  • Airflow, containment, and thermal verification
  • Sequence of operations validation
  • Controls and BMS point to point verification

Integrated Testing and QA/QC

Whole facility performance verified under failure conditions.

  • Integrated systems testing across electrical and mechanical
  • Utility failure and failover scenarios under load
  • Test script development, review, and completeness auditing
  • Specification and submittal conflict resolution
  • Issue tracking, punch, and closeout documentation
Process

The commissioning lifecycle

Commissioning spans the construction schedule, from documentation review before installation to integrated testing at completion.

01

Submittal Review

Review specifications and submittals before procurement and installation.

02

Factory Witness

Verify equipment against specification prior to shipment.

03

Construction Verification

Confirm installation is complete and ready for energization.

04

Pre-Energization

Inspect and clear each asset before it is energized.

05

Functional Testing

Verify each system performs to its sequence of operations.

06

Integrated Testing

Test the facility under load and failure conditions.
